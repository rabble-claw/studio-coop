# Customer Development Discovery Script

Use this script for 45-60 minute discovery interviews with studio owners/managers.

## Call Setup

1. "Can I record this with Otter for notes only?"

## Core Questions (Ask Everyone)

1. "What is your role day-to-day, and what software touches your week most?"
2. "Walk me through your actual workflow from class setup to booking to payment to attendance."
3. "Where does this workflow break, create stress, or cost time?"
4. "What do Mindbody, Glofox, or similar tools do that feels painful or frustrating?"
5. "What makes you consider switching platforms, and what stops you?"
6. "How bad is inbound sales spam in your world? What channels and how often?"
7. "Who do you actually trust when evaluating new software?"
8. "If another studio owner recommended a platform, what would make that credible?"
9. "What onboarding help would you want from a peer owner vs from the software team?"
10. "What reward model feels fair for referrals: cash, fee credits, or ownership/equity?"
11. "What would make a referral program feel spammy or unethical?"
12. "What proof would you need in 30 days to say: this works, I'd recommend it?"
13. "Would you introduce us to 1-2 owners if we deliver that proof?"

## Emma Add-On (Active Studio Owner)

1. "What does a perfect Studio Manager/Owner flow look like in your studio?"
2. "What are the biggest workflow differences between owner, teacher, and student?"
3. "Where do teachers or students currently get confused or blocked?"

## Daniela Add-On (Former Owner)

1. "Looking back, what operational bottlenecks hurt Endorphinz most?"
2. "What software/reporting/payments issues made survival harder?"
3. "What would a platform have needed to do to materially change the outcome?"

Cooperative Principles

We operate in accordance with the International Cooperative Alliance (ICA) values and principles:

1. Voluntary & Open Membership Any fitness or wellness studio can join, without discrimination.
2. Democratic Member Control One studio, one vote. Members elect the Board and set policy.
3. Member Economic Participation Members contribute equitably and benefit from surplus in proportion to their use.
4. Autonomy & Independence The cooperative is controlled by its members, not outside investors or venture capital.
5. Education & Training We provide education for members, staff, and the public about cooperative principles.
6. Cooperation Among Cooperatives We work with other cooperatives to strengthen the cooperative movement.
7. Concern for Community We contribute to the sustainable development of the communities our studios serve.

Membership Structure

Studio Co-operative Limited is a multi-stakeholder cooperative with three classes of members:

Fitness and wellness studios that subscribe to the platform. Studio Members are the primary stakeholders ‚Äî the cooperative exists to serve them. Each studio gets one vote regardless of size.

Why a Cooperative?

Fitness studios depend on their management platform. When that platform is owned by venture capital, the incentives don't align ‚Äî prices go up, features get paywalled, and studios have no say.

A cooperative fixes this. When studios own the platform, the platform works for them. No outside investors demanding growth at all costs. No surprise price hikes. No data exploitation. Just a tool built by and for the studios that use it.

This is what .coop means: real cooperative ownership, not just branding.
